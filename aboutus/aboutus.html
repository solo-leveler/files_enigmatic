About us

Enigmatic Plodder is the Official Quad Bike team of SRM Institute Of Technology, Kattankulathur, Chennai. We are a student run, non-profit team specialising in the development, design and fabrication of All-Terrain Vehicles also known as Quad Bikes, and take part in national competitions namely Quad Bike Design Challenge and Quad Torc.

The biggest wildfires require all but a spark to set it ablaze. This spark was present within Vishal Kumar, a passionate engineer who along with his team, under the guidance of the Team Principal, Dr. Leenus Jesu Martin along with the team's Faculty Advisor Mr. S. Madhan Kumar, formed the team back in 2014. Enigmatic Plodder is result of zeal and sheer determination of engineering students that wanted to create a difference. We have moved forward a long way, to what we are today. Our aims comprise of manufacturing products that are comparable to the standards set by the pre-established companies. Together we look to provide our engineering fundamentals with a practical approach in order to build a quad bike. As a team we look to further progress, through innovations and technologies to produce better products with time to come.
