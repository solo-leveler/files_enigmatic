PHOENIX

Phoneix was fabricated in the year 2019. The vehicle took part in the Quad The Off Road Chamionship, organised by the Indian Society of New Era Enigneers (ISNEE).






Design

Width - 1283.0mm
Length - 1727.2mm
Wheelbase - 1100mm
Trackwidth - 1080mm
Weight - 190KG

Engine

FZ 250cc, Air cooled and Four Ring Oil cooled,
Power - 20.9HP @ 8000rpm
Torque - 20Nm @ 6000rpm

Wheels

Front wheel - 23x8-12
Rear wheel - 23x10-12

Transmission

Type - Single reduction chain-drive transmission

Suspension Type

Front - Double wishbone
Rear - H-Arm wishbone(Damper to lower wishbone) and single upper link.

Brakes

Type - Hydraulic disc brakes front and rear

Steering

Type - 4 bar linkage centralised point
