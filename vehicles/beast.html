BEAST

Beast was fabricated in the year 2017. The vehicle took part in the Quad Bike Design Challenge 2017, organised by the Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers (FMAE).






Design

Width - 1283.0mm
Length - 1727.2mm
Wheelbase - 1100mm
Trackwidth - 1080mm
Weight - 199KG

Engine

CBR 250cc, Liquid cooled,
Power - 26.5BHP @ 8500rpm
Torque - 22.9Nm @ 7000rpm

Wheels

Front wheel - 23x8-12
Rear wheel - 23x12-12

Transmission

Type - Single reduction chain-drive transmission

Suspension Type

Front - Double wishbone
Rear - Trailing arm

Brakes

Type - Hydraulic disc brakes front and rear

Steering

Type - 4 bar linkage centralised point
