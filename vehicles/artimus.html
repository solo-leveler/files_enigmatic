ARTEMIS

Artemis was fabricated in the year 2016. The vehicle took part in Quad Torc 2016, organised by Indian Society of New Era Engineers (ISNEE).






Design

Width - 1247.8mm
Length - 1620.7mm
Wheelbase - 1100mm
Trackwidth - 960mm
Weight - 160Kg

Engine

Lifan 250cc, Air cooled,
Power - 11.5KW @ 7000rpm
Torque - 16Nm @6000rpm

Wheels

Front wheel - 22x7-10
Rear wheel - 22x10-9

Transmission

Type - Double reduction transmission with intermediate jackshaft

Suspension Type

Front - Double wishbone
Rear - Trailing arm

Brakes

Type - Hydraulic disc brakes front and rear

Steering

Type - 4 bar linkage centralised point
