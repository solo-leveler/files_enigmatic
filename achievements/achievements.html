The team at present has manufactured 5 running vehicles over the years of its functioning. Each successive vehicle has come with changes and significant improvements over its predecessor. These changes have been implemented to ensure the constant evolution of the team. The team has taken part in SIX competitions: - Quad Torc 2015, organised by the Indian Society of New Era Engineers (ISNEE) - Quad Torc 2016, organised by the Indian Society of New Era Engineers (ISNEE) - Quad Bike Design Challenge 2017, organised by the Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers (FMAE) - Quad Bike Design Challenge 2018, organised by the Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers (FMAE) - Quad Torc 2018, organised by the Indian Society of New Era Engineers (ISNEE) - Quad Bike Design Challenge 2019, organised by the Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers (FMAE)
